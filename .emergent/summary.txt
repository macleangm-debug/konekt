**Key Technical Concepts**
-   **Partner Ecosystem:** A model where Konekt collaborates with a hidden network of third-party suppliers and service providers for fulfillment.
-   **Hidden Supplier Model:** An architectural principle where the identity and details of the fulfillment partner are never exposed to the end customer. Konekt orchestrates the process and owns the customer relationship.
-   **Geographical Routing:** Logic to determine the optimal fulfillment source (internal or partner) based on the customer's country and region, combined with business rules.
-   **Localized Pricing:** A dynamic pricing engine where the final customer price is calculated from a partner's base price plus country- and category-specific markups controlled by Konekt's admin.
-   **Split Fulfillment:** The process of breaking down a single customer order into multiple internal fulfillment jobs, one for each unique supplier/source, to be tracked independently.
